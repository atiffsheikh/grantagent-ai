What is GrantAgent AI?

A four-agent AI system that helps nonprofits find matching grants and generate application drafts. Built in partnership with - Philadelphia using real grant data. The system handles discovery, org profiling, eligibility scoring, and draft generation — cutting 10–20 hours of manual work per application down to minutes. New: Outline Mode produces structured topic guides instead of full AI prose, so grant writers never miss a required focus area while writing in their own voice.

Beyond Nonprofits

The core architecture — scraping structured opportunity data, building entity profiles, scoring fit, and generating tailored responses — generalizes well beyond grant writing. The same matching pipeline applies to corporate philanthropy programs pairing with community partners, sales teams matching prospects to products, and infrastructure or vendor selection workflows where requirements need to be scored against a large pool of candidates.
